Commercial Interior Projects

Designing functional, brand-forward workspaces that inspire productivity

Elevate Your Business Environment

Commercial spaces are more than workplaces—they're brand experiences. Whether you're launching a startup office, renovating a retail showroom, or designing a restaurant, we create interiors that align with your brand identity while prioritizing functionality, employee well-being, and customer experience.

Corporate Offices

Modern offices balance collaboration and focus. We design open-plan workstations, private cabins, meeting rooms, and breakout zones that boost productivity and reflect your company culture. Ergonomic furniture, acoustic solutions, and smart lighting are standard.

  • Reception and lobby design
  • Workstation layouts and partitions
  • Conference rooms with AV integration
  • Pantry and lounge areas
  • Branding through colors and graphics

Retail Showrooms

Retail interiors must attract, engage, and convert. We design showrooms with strategic product placement, eye-catching displays, and smooth customer flow. Lighting, signage, and trial areas are optimized to enhance the shopping experience.

  • Window displays and storefront design
  • Product shelving and display units
  • Trial rooms and cash counter design
  • Lighting to highlight merchandise
  • Flooring and wall treatments

Restaurants & Cafés

Dining spaces create memories. We craft ambiences—from cozy cafés to upscale fine-dining restaurants—that complement your menu and brand. Kitchen layouts, seating comfort, acoustics, and décor all contribute to a memorable dining experience.

  • Seating layouts (booths, tables, bar counters)
  • Kitchen and service area planning
  • Theme-based décor and styling
  • Outdoor dining and terrace design
  • Lighting and music ambience control

Hotels & Hospitality

Hotels demand luxury, comfort, and operational efficiency. We design guest rooms, lobbies, banquet halls, and spa areas that deliver five-star experiences. Every touchpoint—from check-in to room service—is enhanced through thoughtful design.

  • Guest room and suite interiors
  • Lobby and reception area design
  • Banquet and event spaces
  • Spa, gym, and wellness centers
  • Restaurant and bar design within hotels

Clinics & Healthcare

Healthcare interiors prioritize hygiene, comfort, and patient experience. We design clinics, diagnostic centers, and dental offices with calming colors, efficient layouts, and easy-to-clean surfaces. Waiting areas, consultation rooms, and treatment zones are optimized.

  • Reception and waiting area design
  • Consultation and examination rooms
  • Sterilization and storage planning
  • ADA-compliant and accessible design
  • Calming interiors for patient comfort

Educational Institutions

Schools, coaching centers, and training institutes need inspiring, functional spaces. We design classrooms, libraries, labs, and staff rooms that encourage learning and collaboration. Durable materials, ergonomic furniture, and vibrant colors create engaging environments.

  • Classroom furniture and layout
  • Library and reading room design
  • Computer and science lab interiors
  • Auditorium and assembly hall design
  • Play areas and activity zones
