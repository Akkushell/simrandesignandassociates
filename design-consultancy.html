Design Consultancy Services

Expert guidance to bring clarity and confidence to your interior design journey

Professional Design Advice, Tailored for You

Not every project requires full-scale execution. Sometimes, you just need expert guidance—a design roadmap, material recommendations, or 3D visualizations to make informed decisions. Our design consultancy services empower homeowners, builders, and businesses with professional insights without the commitment of full-service execution.

Concept & Layout Design

We start with understanding your space, functional needs, and aesthetic preferences. Our team develops concept plans—floor layouts, furniture arrangements, and spatial flow diagrams—that maximize utility and beauty. You receive detailed CAD drawings and design narratives.

  • Space planning and furniture layout
  • Traffic flow and zoning analysis
  • Room-by-room functional planning
  • CAD drawings (2D floor plans, elevations)
  • Design mood boards and style direction

3D Visualization & Rendering

See your space come to life before execution. Our photorealistic 3D renderings show exactly how colors, materials, lighting, and furniture will look. This eliminates guesswork and helps you finalize decisions with confidence. Multiple design options can be explored virtually.

  • Photorealistic 3D interior renders
  • Multiple angle views and walkthroughs
  • Material and color experimentation
  • Day and night lighting simulations
  • Revision rounds for fine-tuning

Material Consultation

Choosing the right materials is crucial for durability, maintenance, and aesthetics. We guide you through flooring, wall finishes, countertops, tiles, fabrics, and hardware. Our recommendations balance quality, budget, and design intent, and we connect you with trusted suppliers.

  • Flooring (wood, laminate, tiles, vinyl)
  • Wall finishes (paint, wallpaper, cladding)
  • Countertops (granite, quartz, marble)
  • Fabric and upholstery selection
  • Hardware, fixtures, and accessories

Color Scheme Development

Colors set the mood. We create harmonious color palettes tailored to your space, lighting conditions, and personal preferences. Whether bold and vibrant or calm and neutral, we ensure cohesion across rooms and finishes.

  • Room-specific color palettes
  • Accent wall and feature color selection
  • Coordination with furniture and décor
  • Paint brand and finish recommendations
  • Lighting impact on color perception

Furniture & Décor Sourcing

Finding the right furniture can be overwhelming. We curate furniture pieces, lighting fixtures, art, and accessories that align with your design vision and budget. We can provide sourcing lists or accompany you for vendor visits.

  • Furniture style and size recommendations
  • Vendor and showroom references
  • Custom furniture design suggestions
  • Lighting fixture selection
  • Art, rugs, and accessory curation

Vastu & Compliance Guidance

For clients seeking Vastu-compliant designs or adherence to building codes, we provide consultancy that respects traditional principles while maintaining modern aesthetics. We also ensure designs meet safety and accessibility standards.

  • Vastu Shastra principles integration
  • Building code and fire safety compliance
  • Accessibility (ADA) guidelines
  • Energy-efficient design recommendations
  • Sustainable material suggestions

Who Benefits from Design Consultancy?

DIY Enthusiasts

Homeowners managing their own projects benefit from professional layouts, material advice, and design validation to avoid costly mistakes.

Builders & Developers

Real estate developers seeking design inputs for model flats, sample homes, or spec projects gain competitive edge with our expertise.

Renovators

Updating parts of your home? Consultancy helps you refresh spaces cohesively without full-scale redesign, saving time and money.

Budget-Conscious Clients

Consultancy is a cost-effective way to access professional design expertise while retaining control over execution and vendor selection.

Our Consultancy Process

01

Initial Consultation

We meet to discuss your project scope, goals, constraints, and budget. Site assessment (in-person or virtual) is conducted.

02

Design Development

Our team prepares layouts, mood boards, 3D renders, or material palettes based on your chosen consultancy package.

03

Presentation & Feedback

We present the design and incorporate your feedback through revision rounds to refine the final output.

04

Handover & Support

You receive all design files, material specifications, and sourcing lists. Post-handover support is available for clarifications.
