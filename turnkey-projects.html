Turnkey Interior Projects

Complete end-to-end solutions—from concept to final handover

One Team. One Responsibility. Zero Hassle.

Turnkey interior projects are our specialty. We handle everything—design, procurement, execution, and styling—so you don't have to juggle multiple vendors or worry about coordination. From the first sketch to the final reveal, we take complete ownership, delivering your dream space on time and within budget.

Design & Planning

Every turnkey project begins with comprehensive planning. We develop detailed designs, 3D renders, material specifications, and project timelines. You approve every element before execution begins, ensuring complete transparency and alignment.

  • Site measurement and analysis
  • Concept development and mood boards
  • Photorealistic 3D visualization
  • Detailed working drawings (floor plans, elevations, sections)
  • Material and color finalization

Material Procurement

We source all materials—flooring, tiles, paint, wallpaper, hardware, fabrics, and more—from trusted suppliers. Our procurement team ensures quality checks, timely delivery, and cost optimization. You benefit from our vendor relationships and bulk pricing.

  • Flooring (tiles, wood, laminate, vinyl)
  • Wall finishes (paint, wallpaper, stone cladding)
  • False ceiling materials (gypsum, POP, metal)
  • Lighting fixtures and electrical accessories
  • Hardware, fittings, and sanitary ware

Civil & Structural Work

Turnkey projects often involve structural modifications—partition walls, ceiling work, plumbing, and electrical rewiring. Our team coordinates with civil contractors to execute these works safely, adhering to building codes and timelines.

  • Partition walls and demolitions
  • False ceiling installation
  • Electrical and plumbing routing
  • Flooring and tiling work
  • Painting and wall treatments

Custom Furniture & Carpentry

Built-in wardrobes, TV units, modular kitchens, and custom furniture are crafted to fit your space perfectly. We design, manufacture, and install carpentry with premium materials and finishes, ensuring durability and style.

  • Modular kitchens (cabinets, countertops, appliances)
  • Wardrobes and walk-in closets
  • TV units and entertainment centers
  • Shoe racks, lofts, and storage solutions
  • Study tables, vanities, and pooja units

Lighting Design

Lighting transforms spaces. We design layered lighting schemes—ambient, task, and accent—using ceiling lights, wall sconces, LED strips, and spotlights. Smart lighting controls and dimmers add convenience and mood flexibility.

  • Ceiling lights (chandeliers, pendants, recessed)
  • Wall-mounted and task lighting
  • LED strip and cove lighting
  • Outdoor and garden lighting
  • Home automation integration (optional)

Finishing & Styling

The final touches make all the difference. We style your space with furniture, soft furnishings, art, plants, and accessories. Our team ensures every detail—from curtain rods to cushion placement—reflects the design vision.

  • Furniture selection and placement
  • Curtains, blinds, and upholstery
  • Wall art, mirrors, and décor items
  • Indoor plants and planters
  • Final cleaning and handover

Why Choose Turnkey Solutions?

Single Point of Contact

No need to coordinate with multiple vendors. Our project manager is your single point of contact, handling everything from design approvals to site supervision.

Time & Cost Efficiency

Turnkey projects are faster and often more economical. We optimize workflows, leverage vendor relationships, and prevent delays through proactive planning.

Quality Assurance

We maintain strict quality control at every stage—material inspections, workmanship audits, and final checks ensure flawless execution.

Stress-Free Experience

You focus on your daily life; we manage the project. Regular updates, transparent billing, and professional execution make the journey smooth.
